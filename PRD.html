1. Product Overview

FlatFlow is a full-stack Apartment Society Management web application that digitizes residential operations in India. It provides separate dashboards for Residents and Admins, enabling management of complaints, payments, visitors, and analytics.

1.1 Vision Statement

"To replace paper-based registers and manual collection with a beautiful, secure digital platform."
